Starting XI: Wits v SuperSport

08 Nov 2019, 18:49 by Soccer Laduma

Absa Premiership

Teams: Bidvest Wits v SuperSport United

Date: 08 November 2019

Venue: Bidvest Stadium

Bidvest Wits are back in Absa Premiership action this evening with the visit of SuperSport United at the Bidvest Stadium.

View The PSL Table

The Clever Boys were last in involved in league matters back in October when they Chippa United 1-0 in Port Elizabeth.

Despite their involvement in the CAF Confederation qualifiers, Wits still find themselves in third spot having only played six league games this season.

The Clever Boys do come into the clash in fine form after thrashing U.D Songo 6-0 last Sunday to book their spot in the Confederation Cup group stages.

SuperSport, meanwhile, have not tasted victory in their last four encounters across all competitions, with the last encounter ending in a goalless draw with Golden Arrows on Wednesday night.

Kaitano Tembo's charges sit in fourth spot, one point adrift of the Clever Boys, although neither side could go higher than third with a win this evening.
