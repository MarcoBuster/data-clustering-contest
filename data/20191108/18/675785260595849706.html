Brutal warning by Mexican cartel sent to rivals in bloody drug war

WARNING - GRAPHIC IMAGES: The Jalisco New Generation Cartel (CJNG) sent horrifying pictures of dismembered corpses to Los Viagras, The Michoacan Family, and the Knights Templar as a threat

08 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Tom Davidson, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/tom-davidson/

A brutal Mexican cartel sent horrifying images to their rivals as a warning.

The Jalisco New Generation Cartel (CJNG) posted the pictures on social media as part of a threat against Los Viagras, The Michoacan Family, and the Knights Templar.

The four cartels are currently battling for control over the state of Michoacan in western Mexico.

One of the pictures shows a pile of human remains with the message: “Are you coming for more?”

In another, a cartel member holds up a decapitated head with a leg below it.

One video shows CJNG killers firing guns from an armoured vehicle.

It was shared with the message: “We don’t even feel tickles from Los Viagras and Los Templarios.

“Pick up your dead people, we are going to leave them for you at the La Barrenada lake so you return them back to their families.

“Don’t be b******s, don’t tell them that they disappeared.

“B******s do not send children to fight, they are cannon fodder, there are 100 of you Viagras and Templarios against 20 CJNG but you don’t do anything.”

Another clip shows CJNG hitmen in a forested area carrying guns saying: “We are going to kill you like the b******s you are, f*****g pigs”.

The video was reportedly recorded in an area known as Tierra Caliente by the CJNG lieutenant Miguel Fernandez.

Last night, it emerged that a police officer who had arrested the son of El Chapo was shot more than 100 times in what appeared to be an assassination.

Authorities were later forced to release Guzman to avoid further bloodshed.
