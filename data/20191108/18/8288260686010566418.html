'Aiming higher' - Rangers fans have their say on Aston Villa's January transfer plan

Aston Villa transfer news | The Colombian is in a rich vein of form for Rangers and could be in-demand when the window re-opens in 2020

08 Nov 2019, 18:45 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Rangers fans have given their verdict after Aston Villa boss Dean Smith name checked Alfredo Morelos.

The Gers striker has been extensively linked with a switch to B6, with Smith in the market for more goal scoring reinforcements.

The 23-year-old is in blistering form for Steven Gerrard's side and added to his collection with a strike from distance in their Europa League win over Porto.

The Villa boss was asked about a move for Morelos and refused to be drawn on the speculation: "I certainly saw his goal last night," he siad.

"It was a very good finish.

"He's a player who is contracted to another football club and we aren't a club that talks about them."

 

Although he remained coy on links with Morelos he did all but confirm they would look to sign a striker in the January window.

"Possibly, simply because Keinan Davis has a hamstring injury at the moment so he's one who is out," Smith added.

"We've got young Wesley who is playing his first Premier League campaign, like a few others are, so we will just have to wait and see until January."
