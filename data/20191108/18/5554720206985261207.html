Bigg Boss house sees fresh fight between Rashami Desai and Siddharth Shukla, Shehnaaz Gill lectured on selfishness

08 Nov 2019, 18:36 by JKR Staff

Bigg Boss house once again erupted in an ugly exchange of words between two traditional rivals namely Siddharth Shukla and Rashami Desai. This was the first time the two contestants had locked horns after Rashami’s eviction from the reality show last week.

The fight started over the allocation of duties after the house elected their new captain since Aarti Singh’s tenure had come to an end. Himanshi Khurana and Shefali Jariwala were tied with the same number of votes. The last week’s captain Aarti was then asked to select the new captain between the two.

Siddharth took objection to Rashami bringing his family into the verbal fight. When Rashami’s friend Arhaan Khan came to her defence, Siddharth decided to have a go at him too. He did not even spare Paras Chhabra after the latter was seen siding with Rashami.

The show also saw a brief argument between Siddharth and Tehseen Poonawalla. Siddharth was later seen poking fun at Tehseen questioning his ability to become a successful politician with such an attitude. He was seen questioning his ability secure a single vote with such an attitude.
