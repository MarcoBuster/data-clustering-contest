Young boy airlifted to hospital after Kilmarnock horror crash

Police attended the scene and closed the road between Campbeltown Drive and Hill Street.

08 Nov 2019, 18:36 by Kathleen Speirs

A young boy is in hospital after being hit by a vehicle in Kilmarnock.

Emergency services raced to Western Road after the alarm was raised at around 3.10pm on Friday.

Police attended the scene and closed the road between Campbeltown Drive and Hill Street.

 

The young boy was airlifted to Crosshouse Hospital .

Medics describe his condition as currently stable.

A spokesperson from Police Scotland said: ""We were called to a collision at around 3.10pm.

"The road was closed southbound between Hill Street and Campbeltown Drive.

"Enquiries are ongoing."

 

The stretch of road was re-opened at around 4.20pm ahead of evening rush-hour traffic.
