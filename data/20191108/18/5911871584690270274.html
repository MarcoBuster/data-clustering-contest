Shut Up, Dude: This Week’s Best And Worst Comments

08 Nov 2019, 18:02 by Scott Lapatine

This week we ran our 2010s In Review features, and thank god that’s over! We put a ton of work into it, only narrowly avoiding a mutiny. It’s been fun reading your lists in the comments — will have to check out this Kaputt guy. Anyway, while our lists were of course perfecto, keep in mind when you have a whole staff of music writers trying to come to a consensus, a lot of the more outré picks will fall by the wayside. It doesn’t mean most of us don’t love doom metal or whatever.

Beyond our 100 Best Albums and 200 Best Songs lists, we looked back at the decade in music videos, rap music, and one-hit wonders. We also named Kendrick Lamar Artist Of The Decade which I’m surprised any of you could disagree with!
