Bucs Get Bokke Inspiration Ahead Of Soweto Derby

08 Nov 2019, 18:00 by SL Social

With the Springboks returning home from their Rugby World Cup-winning exploits, the men in green and gold took some time out of their celebrations to visit the Orlando Pirates camp. Check it out!

Earlier this week, fans flocked to the OR Tambo International Airport to welcome the South African national rugby team back in full voice.

After their trophy parade tour of Pretoria, coach Rassie Erasmus and his troops made their way to one of Pirates' training sessions, where they were greeted with much pride by the Bucs stars.
