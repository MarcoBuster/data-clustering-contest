Listen: John Legend, Kelly Clarkson's version of 'Baby It's Cold Outside' drops

08 Nov 2019, 18:34 by Natalie Dreier, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

The remake of the Christmas song "Baby It's Cold Outside" has dropped, and now fans can hear the new, post #MeToo lyrics for themselves.

John Legend posted the song to Twitter to allow fans to hear the new track co-written by Natasha Rothwell and sung by Legend and Kelly Clarkson.
