Judge Indicted

08 Nov 2019, 18:12 by BRIEF

HOUSTON — Harris County (Houston) Judge Alexandra Smoots-Thomas surrendered to federal agents Friday to face charges she used campaign contributions to pay her mortgage, travel and buy luxury merchandise, federal prosecutors said.
