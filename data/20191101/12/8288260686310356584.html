Aston Villa latest | Dean Smith’s men welcome the Premier League leaders to Villa Park on Saturday (3pm) as they look to end their long unbeaten run in the league

01 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Jonny Bonell

Aston Villa welcome Premier League leaders Liverpool to Villa Park tomorrow (Saturday).

Dean Smith’s men will be looking for a fourth win since their return to the top flight, while the Reds are looking to extend their unbeaten run at the start of the season.

Liverpool are already six points clear of nearest challengers Manchester City, having dropped points in just one game this season – a draw away to Manchester United.
