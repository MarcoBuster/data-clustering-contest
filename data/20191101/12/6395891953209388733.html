1 seriously injured in Roxbury shooting

01 Nov 2019, 12:19 by EndPlay

BOSTON - One person has life-threatening injuries after a shooting near Cedar and Centre streets in Roxbury late Thursday.

Boston police said the man was shot just before midnight and was rushed to a local hospital with life-threatening injuries. Another man, who police believe was stabbed, sustained non-life threatening injuries.

Police, who spent hours overnight talking with potential witnesses and collecting evidence, have not said what led to the violence.

Officials have not made any arrests and it's unclear if they have any suspects.

Police said the investigation is ongoing.
