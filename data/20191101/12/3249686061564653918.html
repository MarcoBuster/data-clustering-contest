IU tops the charts with latest song 'Love Poem'!

01 Nov 2019, 12:33 by crisis911

IU has just released her latest song and it has already made some buzz! 

On November 1 at 6 p.m. KST, the breathtaking star shared “Love Poem,” a pre-release song for her upcoming album of the same name. By 8 p.m. KST, the song had taken No. 1 on all five major music charts: Melon, Genie, Bugs, Soribada, and Flo. 

Congratulations IU! 

The star is all geared up for her upcoming tour 'Love, Poem' which starts tomorrow November 2nd.
