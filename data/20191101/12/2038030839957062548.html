WATCH: Here's what you can expect from this Sunday's episode of 'Trackers'

01 Nov 2019, 12:40

Cape Town - The thrilling pilot episode of the five-part series Trackers, based on Deon Meyer’s international bestselling novel, received rave reviews across the African continent.

Expect even more heart-thumping action scenes, brilliant acting and edge-of-your-seat moments in episode 2.

Finding sexy rhino vet Flea - who pulled the wool over many eyes before disappearing with a stash of diamonds - becomes a matter of life or death for several characters. And, who is the international man of mystery who was carjacked after arriving in Cape Town?
