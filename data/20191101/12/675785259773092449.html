BREAKING Stoke sack Nathan Jones after dismal start to the season

The Potters are second bottom of the Championship table after just two league wins all season

01 Nov 2019, 12:15 by Jacob Murtagh, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/jacob-murtagh/

Stoke have axed manager Nathan Jones after a disastrous start to the campaign.

The struggling Potters have lost 10 of their 14 matches and sit 23rd in the Championship table after just two wins all season.

Departing boss Jones said: “I would like to thank John and Peter Coates, firstly for the opportunity to manage this Football Club and then for the continued support and patience throughout my time here.

"I would also like to thank the supporters for their fantastic support. Finally, I wish the Club the very best for the future.”
