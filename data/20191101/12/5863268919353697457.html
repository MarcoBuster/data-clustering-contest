CM Buzdar orders indiscriminate action against criminals

01 Nov 2019, 12:17 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – Punjab Chief Minister Sardar Usman Buzdar has on Friday ordered to take indiscriminate action against criminals, to ensure public protection and the rule of law across the province.

Usman Buzdar chaired a session in Lahore and monitored the law and order situation in Punjab. The chief minister was briefed on the measures taken in this regard.

Provincial minister Muhammad Hashim Dogar, Punjab Inspector General of Police (IGP) Captain (retd) Arif Nawaz Khan, Punjab Home Department Additional Chief Secretary, Advocate General Punjab, Principal Secretary to the CM and other concerned authorities were present in the meeting.
