Giannis Antetokounmpo Wishes Malcolm Brogdon ‘Was Still Here’

15 Nov 2019 by Marcel Mutoni

Giannis Antetokounmpo wishes former Bucks teammate Malcolm Brogdon was “still here.”

Instead, Milwaukee’s front-office allowed Brogdon to ink a four-year, $85 million deal with the Indiana Pacers as a restricted free agent last summer.
