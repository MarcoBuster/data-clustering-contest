What is the fate of Tigray? Politicians form the region talk about it

15 Nov 2019

borkena

“What is the fate of Tigray,” as weird as the question may sound, leaders of Tigray people’s Liberation Front(TPLF) have openly disclosed that “there is a secessionist sentiment in the region.”

Other opposition parties in the region,like Arena for example, state that people in Tigray do not have anti-Ethiopian sentiment but the ruling party in the region (TPLF) is working on it.

In the conversation below leaders from Arena Tigray part and Baitona, the newest party in the region share their views.
