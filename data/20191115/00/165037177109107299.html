The favourites to succeed Robert Moreno

15 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Federation are happy with Roberto Moreno's work. However, according to 'Mundo Deportivo', they already have a list of possible replacements. Ernesto Valverde, Marcelino and Luis Enrique would be the RFEF's favourites.

Robert Moreno's future in charge of Spain could come to an abrupt end. The coach was asked about his future in a press conference and he was blunt. "They have not offered me a renewal and I won't be expecting it soon," he said.

The Federation are satisfied with his work. Since Luis Enrique left the job, Robert Moreno took the reigns and the RFEF think that he has exceeded all expectations.

But for the future, 'Mundo Deportivo' states that the Federation has other plans. There are three people who are favourites to be the next Spanish team coach.

Luis Enrique would be one of the favourite options. He had to leave the role after the death of his daughter and the Federation has always trusted in his potential as a coach.

Another of the options that would be considered, always according to the information of the media mentioned above, would be that of Ernesto Valverde. In this case, the Federation would only make a proposal to the him in case he leaves Barca at the end of the season.

Finally, they would also quite like Marcelino García Toral. The coach is currently without a team, after leaving Valencia.

In whichever case, we must wait and see what will happen first with Robert Moreno. The national team coach is focused on his work in getting Spain into the Euro 2020. His future, he's made it clear, doesn't matter right now.
