Olaniyi, Foreman lift Stony Brook past Texas A&M-CC 68-63

15 Nov 2019

CORPUS CHRISTI, Texas (AP) - Elijah Olaniyi scored 19 points as Stony Brook edged past Texas A&M-Corpus; Christi 68-63 on Friday night. Makale Foreman added 17 points for the Seawolves, while Andrew Garcia chipped in 15.

Mouhamadou Gueye had eight rebounds and three blocks for Stony Brook (2-2).

Myles Smith scored a career-high 22 points and had six rebounds for the Islanders (0-3). Jashawn Talton added 12 points. Nolan Bertain had seven rebounds.
