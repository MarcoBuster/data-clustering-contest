U.S: Here’s the NPD sales results for October 2019

15 Nov 2019, 00:06 by ssf1991

We’ve already covered The NPD Group’s hardware data for October 2019, so now it is time to see how the software did. As you probably know by now, Luigi’s Mansion 3 debuted very strongly. It was the Nintendo Switch’s best selling game of the month, it experienced the best debut month that a Luigi’s Mansion title has ever had and it managed to make it to #3 on October’s Top 20 overall chart. Of course, this was not the only Nintendo title to make it on the charts. You can see the chart data down below.
