Bombay Burmah Consolidated September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 3,122.82 crore, up 5.63% Y-o-Y

15 Nov 2019

Reported Consolidated quarterly numbers for Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation are:

Net Sales at Rs 3,122.82 crore in September 2019 up 5.63% from Rs. 2,956.25 crore in September 2018.

Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 184.25 crore in September 2019 down 21.42% from Rs. 234.47 crore in September 2018.

Close

EBITDA stands at Rs. 524.43 crore in September 2019 up 7.2% from Rs. 489.21 crore in September 2018.

Bombay Burmah EPS has decreased to Rs. 26.41 in September 2019 from Rs. 33.60 in September 2018.

Bombay Burmah shares closed at 1,192.45 on November 14, 2019 (NSE) and has given 2.73% returns over the last 6 months and -13.72% over the last 12 months.
