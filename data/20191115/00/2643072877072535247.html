WAEC Releases Nov/Dec Examination Results, See How to Check Your Result

15 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

Naija News reported earlier that the results of candidates who sat for the 2019 West African Senior School Certificate Examination in Nigeria has been released by the West African Examination Council (WAEC).

This was made known by Mr Olu Adenipekun, the Head of National Office, WAEC Nigeria, today at a media conference in Lagos state.

To check your result, follow the instructions below:

  • Go to https://www.waecdirect.org .
  • Enter your WAEC Examination Number in the required column and select your Examination Year
  • Select your Examination Type which is “school candidate result”.
  • Enter the Card Serial Number.
  • Then, Enter the Card PIN
  • Click the “Submit” button to get your 2019 WAEC Result
