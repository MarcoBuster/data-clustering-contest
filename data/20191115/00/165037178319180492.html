Napoli prepare an 'anti Madrid' clause for Fabian

15 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Fabian is on the agenda of many European teams. Without going any further, Madrid has sounded like a possible destination for the footballer. That's why Napoli have been in a hurry and have thought about armouring their star. They want to put a clause on him of 180 million euros.

Napoli's ideal situation would be to offer Fabian a new contract. They want to try and keep hold of him.

According to 'Il Corrierre dello Sport', Napoli want to put a clause of 180 million euros on him. If somebody thinks about trying to get him, they will have to spend a big sum of money.
