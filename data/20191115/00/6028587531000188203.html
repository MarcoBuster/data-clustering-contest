How To Watch Or Stream UFC Fight Night 164: Jan Blachowicz Vs. Ronaldo ‘Jacare’ Souza

15 Nov 2019 by Trent Reinsmith

The UFC visits Brazil for the third and final time in 2019 on Saturday for the ESPN+ streaming UFC Fight Night 164 card. In the main event, Jan Blachowicz welcomes Ronaldo “Jacare” Souza to the light heavyweight division. Blachowicz, who is the No. 6 ranked fighter in the 205-pound division, was on a four-fight winning streak heading into his February matchup against Thiago Santos. Santos won that fight via third-round knockout. That victory earned Santos a shot at light heavyweight champion Jon Jones. Jones defeated the surging Santos via split decision. 

Blachowicz bounced back from that setback, his first knockout loss since Rameau Thierry Sokoudjou defeated him by TKO in 2011 under the KSW banner. In July, Blachowicz scored a second-round knockout win over former UFC middleweight champion Luke Rockhold. After that “Performance of the Night” bonus-winning victory, Blachowicz, who is the former KSW light heavyweight champion, asked to be matched up against Jones. Instead, the UFC decided the 36-year-old should fight another former middleweight in his first contest at light heavyweight.

That fighter is Ronaldo “Jacare” Souza. Souza, who always seemed one fight away from earning a UFC middleweight title shot, went 3-3 in his past six fights at 185 pounds. After a decision loss to Jack Hermansson in April, Souza decided it was time to give the 205-pound division a shot. 

The odds in this matchup favor Blachowicz. 

HOW TO WATCH OR STREAM UFC FIGHT NIGHT 164

UFC Fight Night 164 takes place at Ginasio do Ibirapuera in Sao Paulo, Brazil. The entire card streams on ESPN+. Prelims begin at 5 p.m. ET, followed by the main card at 8 p.m. ET.
