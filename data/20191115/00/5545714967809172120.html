Become a terrible shot with the Fortnite Imperial Stormtrooper skin

Available for a limited time

15 Nov 2019, 00:01 by CJ Andriessen

The next month is going to be absolutely huge for the Star Wars franchise. Not only are we getting the final movie of the Sequel Trilogy next month, but Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is launching on November 15. There is so much Star Wars goodness going on right now that of course the most popular game in the world is getting in on the action.

Epic Games has revealed a new Imperial Stormtrooper skin for Fortnite. The skin is available for purchase in the Item Shop from now until November 17 at 7:00 pm ET. Additionally, if you're purchasing Fallen Order, you can grab the skin for free.
