Smith fastest from Ferrari in MotoE™ FP2

British rider leads the Cup leader by just over a tenth ahead of E-Pole later today

15 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Bradley Smith (One Energy Racing) ended FIM Enel MotoE™ World Cup FP2 at the top of the standings at the Gran Premio Motul de la Comunitat Valenciana. The British rider heads Cup leader Matteo Ferrari (Trentino Gresini MotoE) by 0.168 with Eric Granado (Avintia Esponsorama Racing) the only other rider to dip below the 1:41 barrier.

Smith went over a second quicker than he did in FP1 on Thursday and will now be the man to beat in E-Pole, the number 38 will head out last to try and earn the best grid slot for the races on Saturday and Sunday. Behind the leading trio was FP1 pacesetter Hector Garzo (Tech 3 E-Racing), with Alex de Angelis (Octo Pramac MotoE) completing the top five.

The ever-exciting E-Pole kicks off at 16:05 local time (GMT+1) on Friday afternoon.
