IKEA Unleashes Black Friday Deals For Home Upgrades At A Fraction Of The Price

15 Nov 2019 by Mikelle Leow

IKEA, the wallet-friendly furniture giant, is about to get more affordable this Black Friday. The Swedish company has unveiled its holiday deals, with most of them currently focused on Thanksgiving week.
The deals appear to be applicable only for IKEA Family subscribers, but membership is free, anyway. As an IKEA Family member, you’ll be able to save up to as much as US$200 on Black Friday.
Take a look at some offers available through Black Friday and Cyber Week, which fall between 27 November and 8 December. IKEA’s Black Friday deals landing page should be up by then. Meanwhile, more offers can be found via Refinery29.

Up to US$200 off select sleeper sofas: FRIHETEN, GRÖNLID, and VIMLE

Frames for these sleeper sofas will be US$100 off, while their non-leather fabric covers will be up to US$100 cheaper. All three sections must be purchased to qualify for the deal.

20% off daybeds, including Virgil Abloh’s US$449 MARKERAD Daybed (US$356.20 after discount)
