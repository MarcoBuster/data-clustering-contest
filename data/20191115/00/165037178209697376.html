Gabriel Jesus once again missed from the spot

15 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Gabriel Jesus was the man in charge of taking the penalty which the Brazil earned in the friendly against Argentina. The striker missed the penalty, just like he did for Man City against Atalanta. In fact, the penalties were identical, only that the ball went to the other side this time.

Inside the first 10 minutes in the friendly between the two teams, Pezzella made a terrible error inside his own box and he gave away a penalty. Gabriel Jesus stepped up to take. 

The striker seemed calm, but when it came to hitting it, he sent the ball well wide of the goal with a low and tame effort which untroubled Andrada in the goal. 

It was interesting that the mistake was identical to the terrible spot kick he hit for Man City against Atalanta. That said, he went to the other side on this occasion. The result was the same though: it was a terrible miss.
