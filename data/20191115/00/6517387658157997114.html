Dua Lipa makes it two weeks at the top of the Official Irish Singles Chart with Don’t Start Now

Youtuber, rapper and boxer KSI scores this week's highest new entry.

15 Nov 2019 by Jack White

Dua Lipa is Number 1 of the Official Irish Singles Chart for a second week.

The lead single from Dua’s upcoming second album, Don’t Start Now increased its performance week-on-week to finish 1500 chart sales ahead of the competition. Don’t Start Now has so far reached Number 1 solely in Ireland, peaking at Number 2 in the UK and reaching the Top 10 in Australia, Hungary, Israel and New Zealand.
