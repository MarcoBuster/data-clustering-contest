Taylor Swift’s Conflict With Scooter Braun & Big Machine Records Escalates Amidst Licensing & Royalties Disputes

15 Nov 2019 by Chris Mench

Taylor Swift’s business drama with Scooter Braun and Big Machine Records continues to snowball, and she’s asking fans for help. In a lengthy note posted to her social media on Thursday night, Swift claimed that Braun and Big Machine founder Scott Borchetta have denied her the right to perform any of her old songs at the upcoming American Music Awards, where she will be honored as the Artist of the Decade and was set to perform a medley of her old hits.

She further stated that she likely won’t be able to perform any of her pre-Lover material at any recorded event until her contract expires next November.
