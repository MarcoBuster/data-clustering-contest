Photos: INEC Begins Distribution Of Electoral Materials For Bayelsa Election

15 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) on Friday commenced the distribution of electoral materials for the Bayelsa State Governorship Election.

Naija News reports that INEC had fixed Saturday, November 16, 2019, for the Guber election.

David Lyon of the All Progressives Congress (APC) and Senator Douye Diri of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) are the main contenders for the Bayelsa Guber election.
