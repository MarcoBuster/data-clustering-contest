IVRCL narrows Q2 net loss to Rs 394 cr

Total income during July-September declined to Rs 115.32 crore, as against Rs 245.35 crore in the year-ago period.

15 Nov 2019 by PTI

IVRCL on November 15 reported a standalone net loss of Rs 394.43 crore for the second quarter ended September. The company had posted a net loss of Rs 443.03 crore in the corresponding quarter of the previous fiscal, IVRCL said in a BSE filing.

Total income during July-September declined to Rs 115.32 crore, as against Rs 245.35 crore in the year-ago period.
