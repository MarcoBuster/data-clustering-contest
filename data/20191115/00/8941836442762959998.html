University teams up with Amazon to advance worker IT skills

15 Nov 2019

ADELPHI, Md. (AP) - University of Maryland Global Campus is teaming up with Amazon to advance information technology skills for the retailer’s hourly employees.

The university with a focus on online education says the initiative will launch in January.

It’s designed to prepare employees for industry certifications in IT and associate degrees in related fields through the Amazon Career Choice Program.
