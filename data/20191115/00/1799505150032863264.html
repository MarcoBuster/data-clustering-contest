Scooter Ban

15 Nov 2019, 00:55 by BRIEF

PANAMA CITY BEACH, Fla. – A Florida appeals court ruled that Panama City Beach, Florida, has the right to ban scooter rentals for the safety of the city’s citizens and visitors.
