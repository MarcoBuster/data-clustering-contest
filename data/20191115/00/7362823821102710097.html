Pedestrians looting fish during overturned truck accident goes viral

15 Nov 2019, 00:24 by Gerren Keith Gaynor

A video of people looting live fish strewn across a busy street in Kapur, India, after a truck accident has gone viral.

The video, posted to Twitter on Tuesday, shows a massive crowd of people picking up the fish after a truck overturned in the middle of the road.
