Auto Club NHRA Finals Friday preview

Get all the information on the start of the final race of the 2019 NHRA Mello Yello Drag Racing Series season, the Auto Club NHRA Finals in Pomona

15 Nov 2019 by Jacob Sundstrom, NHRA National Dragster Associate Editor

The final race of the 2019 NHRA Mello Yello Series Countdown to the Championship begins at Auto Club Raceway in Pomona. All four titles are still up for grabs in at the Auto Club NHRA Finals and you can read up on all of the scenarios below.

WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP SCENARIOS

Racing begins in professional categories with Pro Stock and Pro Stock Motorcycle at noon Pacific Time. You can see the full weekend schedule here.

You can catch the action all weekend on FOX Sports 1 and NHRA.tv.

Whether you’re in Pomona, Indianapolis, or anywhere else around the world, you can follow NHRA on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram to get a closer look at the action.
