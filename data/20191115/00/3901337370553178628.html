OFFICIAL: Ruthless England & France Qualify For EURO 2020

15 Nov 2019, 00:13 by SL International

England 7-0 Montenegro

England booked their spot at the 2020 European Championship tournament with a game to spare after hammering Montenegro 7-0 thanks to a hat-trick by Harry Kane in Group A at Wembley.

In addition to Kane's hat-trick, there were also goals from Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain, Marcus Rashford, Tammy Abraham and an own goal by Aleksandar Sofranac, as the Three Lions topped the pool with 18 points.

France 2-1 Moldova

France qualified for the 2020 summer tournament with a game to spare after edging Moldova 2-1 to top Group H at Stade de France.

Vadim Rata gave the visitors a shock early lead but Raphael Varane levelled matters for Les Bleus ten minutes before the break and Olivier Giroud got the winner in the last 11 minutes from the penalty spot.

Turkey 0-0 Iceland

Turkey booked their spot in the 2020 European Championship despite playing to a goalless stalemate with Iceland in Group H at Turk Telekom Stadium.

Turkey qualified with France in second place in the pool, after denying third-placed Iceland the three points and effectively ended their chances of qualifying via the automatic positions.

Serbia 3-2 Luxembourg

Serbia kept their hopes of qualifying alive after edging Luxembourg 3-2 courtesy of a brace by Aleksandar Mitrovic in Group B at Stadion Rajko Mitic.

Mitrovic scored a first-half brace for Serbia, with Nemanja Radojic giving them a 3-1 lead with 20 minutes remaining after Gerson Rodrigues had pulled a goal back for Luxembourg. David Turpel gave the visitors hope to make it 3-2 in the last 15 minutes but the hosts held on to keep their hopes qualifying alive.

Czech Republic 2-1 Kosovo

Czech Republic qualified for the 2020 European Championship tournament after edging direct rivals Kosovo 2-1 in Group A at Doosan Arena.

Atdhe Nuhiu broke the deadlock for Kosovo five minutes after the break but Czechs hit back through Alex Kral in the 71st minute and Ondrej Celustka snatched the winner eight minutes later to seal a place for Czech Republic in the 2020 tournament.
