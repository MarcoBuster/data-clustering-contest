Beckham has a new aim: go for Mandzukic

15 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Mario Mandzukic could try his luck at MLS. This is, atleast, David Beckham's intention. The former English footballer is still sorting out the profile for his new team, Inter Miami, and has thought about the Croatian to lead in attack.

According to 'CalcioMercato', several clubs in the US league are interested in Mandzukic. The first, is David Beckham's new side.

This source states that the team that would have been most interested in having the striker is Inter Miami. However, there has still been no firm offer.
