Watch The Official Video Of Billionaire By Teni

15 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

Talented Nigerian singer, Teni, also known as Teniola Apata, has dished out the official video for her new single, Billionaire.

“Billionaire” is one of the banger singles in the mouth of music lovers in Nigeria.

The 26-year-old singer is known for dishing out the best hit every time she releases a new single.

The superstar single competes on Who Wants To Be A Billionaire a replica of the popular “Who Wants To Be A Millionaire” show.

She was spotted in the video reaching out to her friend, Broda Shaggi when she needed help on the show.

Enjoy the single which was produced by Pheelz and the visual directed by TG Omori.
