Watch now: Taiwanese start-up Starlux Airlines launches its first safety video

15 Nov 2019 by Jackie Chen

Taiwanese start-up carrier Starlux Airlines has unveiled its first ever safety video animated in 3D called “Star Wonderers”.

The safety video, produced on a budget of NTD 30 million (US$0.98 million), features a “Starlux Universe” with a virtual Starlux terminal, aircraft and “Star Wonderers” – passengers from different planets, such as the egg-shaped robot “E-boy”, the “Single-eye Family”, the “Backpacker” that looks like a bag, and music lovers “DJ. Raga” and “Migo”.
