There’s Some Big News About FIVEaa’s On Air Line Up In 2020

15 Nov 2019, 00:02

The return of radio legend Tony Pilkington and the addition of Nova’s Jarrod Walsh headline some exciting line up changes for FIVEaa in 2020.

Jane Reilly also announced on Friday that she will be retiring in 2020 -- doing her last show with FIVEaa Breakfast on Friday 13 December.

According to FIVEаa station manager Craig Munn:

“We at FIVEаa are thrilled to welcome Pilko back home and Jarrod to the station. We believe that our audience will enjoy these additions to the Afternoon line up as well as continue to connect with all our presenters and programs in 2020. For all of us here at FIVEaa, it has been a privilege and delight to work with Jane who is such a passionate and professional South Australian media icon and a treasured and valued member of the FIVEaa family."
