Elon Musk’s Boring Company Starts Digging “Viva Las Vegas Loop”

15 Nov 2019 by Victor Tangermann

Let the Tunneling Begin

In May, the Las Vegas Convention and Visitors Authority (LVCVA) approved a $48.6 million contract with the Boring Company to build an underground transportation system connecting the Las Vegas Convention Center’s massive New Exhibit Hall with the existing campus.

The LCCVC has now announced that the Elon Musk-led tunneling firm officially began digging on Friday — meaning the company is finally working on its first major project.
