Bar Gets In Hot Water For Giant ‘Vulgar’ Door Décor Of A Lady’s Vagina

15 Nov 2019 by Mikelle Leow

A bar in China is being patronized, albeit in a different way, by local authorities and social media users over its “vulgar” doorway decorations.
As reported by Shanghaiist, GT Bar in Ningdu, Jiangxi is going viral for its elaborate, massive setup of inflatable legs in red heels that invite guests to enter its pink “tunnel-like” entrance resembling a woman’s vagina.
Users of Chinese social network Weibo criticized the decorations for demeaning women. Local media outlets also quickly picked up the story, but not without censoring the “entrance” first.

The décor has since been seized and destroyed, and the county’s cultural bureau has proclaimed the bar’s management will be charged with additional administrative penalties over the controversial campaign.
