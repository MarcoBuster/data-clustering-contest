Dell Inspiron 15 5584

The Dell Inspiron 15 offers a nice big display, relatively powerful specs, and it comes at a super affordable price. In other words, if you’re looking for a reliable laptop that will work great for students and professionals alike, this laptop is definitely the way to go.

New Apple MacBook Air

The MacBook Air is the laptop to get if you prefer to stick within Apple’s ecosystem. The laptop is sleek and stylish, plus it’s ultra-portable -- making it a great choice for those who needs something to take on the go. 

It’s a pretty powerful laptop under the hood too. You’ll get an Intel Core i5 processor with 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage. On the edge, there are two Thunderbolt 3 ports -- and that should make the laptop versatile enough to work with any peripheral, as long as you have the right adapters. 

The new MacBook Air normally comes at $1,099.99, but it’s currently available for $984.99.

Lenovo IdeaPad S340

Lenovo makes some of the best laptops for professionals on the go, and the IdeaPad S340 is a perfect example of that, especially for those who need something on a budget. Sure, it’s not the most powerful laptop out there, but it’s still more than enough for most users, especially those mostly using the laptop for productivity apps and similar services.

The laptop comes with an Intel Core i5 processor, along with 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage, which is more than enough processing power for web browsing, word processing, and watching your favorite TV shows and movies. 

The laptop normally comes at $549 -- but it’s currently available for an impressive $389.
