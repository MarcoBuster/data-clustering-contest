Enter 4YFN Barcelona Awards

15 Nov 2019 by Staff reporter

The deadline to apply for the 4YFN Barcelona Awards 2020 – and the chance to win €25K – is 20 November. A dozen startups will be selected to pitch their tech driven solutions on the main stage at 4YFN, with a raft of prizes up for grabs.

Eran Fine, Founder NanoLock Security, the 4YFN Barcelona 2019 Winner, said: “Winning the award has resulted in worldwide exposure and great international coverage. We announced collaborations with Micron and Cyress in the same week and went from unknown company to a recognizable successful startup. My LinkedIn profile exploded in numbers on par with the Kardashians. I’m the Kardashian of the security industry!”

We are media partners with 4YFN Barcelona 2020, which will be held from 24 to 26 February.
