Twist in tale - Meera Mitun gets central government post! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

15 Nov 2019

Actress and self claimed model Meera Mitun had been the centre of controversies as she had entered the Bigg Boss 3 house and had conflicts with almost all contestants and even after the show she had Twitter wars with them.

Meera Mitun was also accused in many financial fraud cases in conducting fashion events and now in a surprise turn of events, she has been appointed by the central government as Tamil Nadu state director, Anti corruption commission. 

Meera revealed it with her Instagram post that read "Corruption, the biggest reason for the downfall of most empires and civilization. Some group of people can tolerate corruption as long as their leader champion their religion or race, some will tolerate bribery as long as they champion their own pockets. I’ve lived long enough shouting anti-corruption slogan against the government having no problem bribing a police officer and vote for a leader of their choice as long as these leaders championed their own race. But here comes the twist and buckle up everyone iam now the Director for Chennai Tamilnadu State in Anti-corruption commission ( A.C.C )and no one can hide hereafter and iam watching. To say in Tamil odavum mudiyathu oliyavum mudiyathu.Join hands with me for a corruption free State."
