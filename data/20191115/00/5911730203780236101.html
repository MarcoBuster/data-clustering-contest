Crash involving pedestrian shuts down southbound lanes of U.S. 19 at Holiday Hills in Pasco County

15 Nov 2019, 00:57 by WFTS Digital Staff

PASCO COUNTY, Fla. -- A pedestrian is in "grave condition" after being hit by a car in Pasco County Thursday night.

The Pasco County Fire Rescue responded to a call of a crash involving a pedestrian in the southbound lanes of U.S. 19 at Holiday Hills. Firefighters say the pedestrian was transported from the scene to the hospital.

Officials did not say what caused the crash. The crash shut down southbound lanes of U.S. 19 in the area.
