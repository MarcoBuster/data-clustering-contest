Actress Shriya Saran has been acting in Tamil and Telugu movies for more than a decade and has acted with top stars of both the industries including Superstar Rajinikanth (Sivaji), Vijay, Vikram, Mahesh Babu, Chiranjeevi and Jr NTR.

The actress who got married last year to her boyfriend, Russian businessman Andrei Koscheev has been constantly in news with her social media posts and photos. After her rain dance and bikini videos few months back, her recent public liplock video with Andrei turned viral.

Now the actress has posted a video where she's seen in enjoying a dip in a swimming pool, and this bikini video of Shriya shot by her mother has been trending in social media. Shriya is said to be the first choice to play Manju Warrier's role in Telugu remake of Asuran, starring Venkatesh.
