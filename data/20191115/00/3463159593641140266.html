CIMED join Brazil 2019 as National Supporter

15 Nov 2019 by FIFA.com

CIMED Indústria de Medicamentos LTDA have come on board for this year’s FIFA U-17 World Cup™. 

With more than 40 years of history, the CIMED Group is one of the top four Brazilian pharmaceutical companies and the only provider with its own national distribution network, serving the more than 50,000 pharmacies in the country.

CIMED’s passion for football led them to become an Official Patron of the Brazilian Football Association in 2016. 

“CIMED have shown they are committed to fostering the next generation of Brazilian talent,” said FIFA Director of Marketing Services Jean-François Pathy. “They share FIFA’s view that youth competitions are a key stepping stone onto the world stage for young athletes, and we are delighted to have their support.”

The final of the FIFA U-17 World Cup Brazil 2019™ will take place on 17 November at the Estádio Bezerrão stadium in Brasília (Gama). For information on attending this weekend’s matches, visit FIFA.com/tickets.
