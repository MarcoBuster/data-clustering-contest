Every drop counts!

15 Nov 2019 by Iain Robertson

Water scarcity is spreading across the planet like wildfire. Gathering pace with every rising degree, it threatens the lives of millions daily. The Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United Nations says each degree of increase causes 500 million more to suffer. Check out the FAO video below to learn more about the solutions it is supporting. And as a business – or individual – take steps to ensure you are making every drop count.
