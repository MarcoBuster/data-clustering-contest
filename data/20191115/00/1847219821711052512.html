Riga’s Vice-Mayor dismisses the board of Riga Central Market

15 Nov 2019 by LETA

Vice-chairman of Riga City Council and shareholder Vadims Baraņņiks has dismissed the board of SIA Riga Central Market.

He explained this decision by saying that Friday, 15 November, is scheduled to hold a meeting of the management to discuss this year’s budget amendments, which were commented on by Riga City Council’s Property Department’s Commercial Operations Office in a negative light.

«The office does not like the board’s approach towards process of drawing up amendments. They also dislike the company is losing payers, because of which there may be losses for a second consecutive years. I received no clear indications how the board plans to resolve this situation,» claims Baraņņiks.

According to him, he offered to board members to assess their responsibility for their posts and submit resignations. However, in response to criticisms, board members left the meeting. As a result of this, the vice-mayor decided to dismiss the board.

Until now Artis Druvinieks was Riga Central Market’s board chairman. Board members included Kaspars Zauls and Valdis Karnītis.
