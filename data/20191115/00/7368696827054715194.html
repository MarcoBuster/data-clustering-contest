Rare Announces Everwild, New Exclusive Game for Xbox One and Windows 10

15 Nov 2019 by Marco Cocomello

Everwild, a new Xbox One and Windows 10 exclusive game was announced last night during the X019 event. Created by the minds behind Banjo-Kazooie, Everywild is a new action-adventure game that looks more like a Breath of the Wild experience than anything else.

The short trailer for Everwild begins with a gorgeous landscape and shows off even more stunning vistas and wildlife. No gameplay is shown as most of the footage is just a bunch of people lurking around but we get the idea that this game is going to see you explore a vast world in search for something.
