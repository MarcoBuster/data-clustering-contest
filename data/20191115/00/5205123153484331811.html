The Killers announce massive open air concert for Dublin's Malahide Castle in summer 2020

15 Nov 2019 by Jack Beresford

THE KILLER will make a long-awaited return to Ireland next year with a massive outdoor gig in Dublin set for summer 2020.

Brandon Flowers and the rest of the band will be bringing their distinctive sound to the scenic surrounds of Malahide Castle in the Irish capital.

The Killers will perform in Dublin on Tuesday, June 16, as part of the band’s stadium tour of the UK and Ireland.

The tour has been put together to support the release of the band’s upcoming new album, Imploding The Mirage.

Due to be released next spring, the album will be The Killers’ sixth to date.

The band will be joined for the Malahide Castle gig by special guest Sam Fender.

Anyone hoping to get their hands on a ticket for the big day will need to be ready.

Tickets go on sale at 9am next Friday, November 22, on Ticketmaster with prices starting at €69.90 including booking fee.
