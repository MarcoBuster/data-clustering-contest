Are you a BOLD innovator?

15 Nov 2019 by Staff reporter

ITALY – Crowdsourcing Week and H-FARM are teaming up again to deliver the second annual Global BOLD Awards. We are media partners with the event taking place in Venice next year, which will recognise innovative breakthroughs in areas including: ‘Open Innovation, Crowdsourcing,  Advertising, Robotics, Virtual and Augmented Reality, Science, Space Frontier, AI, Fintech and Agrotech’.

An international jury of business leaders will have to select the top 60 innovators – from thousands of entries; with a public voting element also part of the awards. Winners will be announced at H-FARM’s campus in Venice on 27 March. For a chance to be there, enter by 31 December, 2019.
