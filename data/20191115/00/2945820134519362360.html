Garzo fastest in MotoE™ FP1

Spaniard leads Smith as the title-deciding weekend kicks off in Valencia

15 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Tech 3 E-Racing’s Hector Garzo kicked off the final FIM Enel MotoE™ World Cup weekend off by topping FP1 at the Gran Premio Motul de la Comunitat Valenciana. The Spaniard’s 1:41.557 was enough to see off second-fastest Bradley Smith (One Energy Racing) by just over two tenths, with Cup leader Matteo Ferrari third quickest on board his Trentino Gresini MotoE Energica Ego Corsa.

The MotoE™ riders will be on track for FP2 at 11:50 local time (GMT+1) on Friday, with E-Pole getting going at 16:05.
