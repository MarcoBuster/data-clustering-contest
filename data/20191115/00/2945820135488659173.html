Applications for the Northern Talent Cup now OPEN!

A new path on the Road to MotoGP™ begins in 2020, and the journey starts now

15 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Applications for the Northern Talent Cup are now OPEN! The newest phase of the Road to MotoGP™ programme starts now, aiming to give riders from northern and central European countries a new opportunity to further their careers and follow their dream of being a motorcycle racer.

A Cup for riders and teams on identical KTM machinery, the NTC aims to provide the perfect stepping stone between lower level national championships and the FIM CEV Repsol and/or Red Bull MotoGP Rookies Cup - and you can now register and begin your NTC journey.
