"We haven't had a simple start, we gave away points"

15 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Striker, Luis Suarez was questioned by FC Barcelona's official media. The Uruguayan remembered his arrival at Barcelona, his best goal, his friendship with Messi and reviewed how the start of the season has gone.

Luis Suarez continues to be a fixed striker for Ernesto Valverde, and one of the predators of the club, as he is always looking for goals. The Uruguayan has already scored eight goals in 13 games and has had around 1,000 minutes of game time.

"The football player is under constant pressure, but that's what we're here for. When you arrive at Barcelona, you hope to win titles, but there are also difficult moments that help you to grow and improve. You have to be prepared, not only on a physical level, but also on a mental one," he expressed.

Regarding the more near future, Luis Suarez hopes for "a great month", then the year will finish with the postponed 'El Clasico'Â  and two Champions League duels.

"We haven't had a simple start, we gave away points that other years we would not have lost, but we know that the rest of the teams also compete. We hope to overcome it with the support of the fans, who have been there for a long time," he continued.
