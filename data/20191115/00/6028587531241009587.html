Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Best Women’s Sunglasses

15 Nov 2019 by Celia Shatzman

Finding just the right holiday present for the fashion lover in your life can come with more than a few challenges. Will she really wear that? Will it fit? With sunglasses, you never have worry about getting the right size. And with endless options to choose from—starting with the classics all the way to the avant-garde—there’s a pair out there for everyone. These are the best women’s sunglasses to gift this holiday season.

Alexander McQueen Jeweled Spider Round Frame, $790, alexandermcqueen.com. Though the lenses may be purple, these stunning sunnies aren’t for shrinking violets. They’re practically jewelry for your face, thanks to the crystal-embellished spider, which plays nicely off of the gradient lenses and delicate gold frames.  

Haze Collection Voz in Tokyo Tortoise, $365, hazecollection.com. These aren’t your momma’s cat eye glasses! Delightfully quirky, we love the juxtaposition of the classic tortoiseshell with the mesh, contrasted with bright blue lenses. The lucky recipient will never get bored of them.

Krewe Blair, $275, krewe.com. It’s hip to be square, as these geometric frames show. The New Orleans-based sunglasses line teamed up with fellow Southerner Bill Reid on a capsule collection of limited edition styles, and this is one of the six offerings. The frame boasts stainless steel construction with flat nylon lenses.  

Gucci Round Acetate Sunglasses, $650, gucci.com. Even the most basic tee and jeans outfit will be instantly elevated with these statement sunnies. Inspired by the round frames of the 1960s, they get Guccified with bold stripes rimmed in metal that add to their vintage vibes. The phrase “Maison de l'Amour” is a motto the fashion house uses regularly to adorn clothing and accessories.  

Stella McCartney Shiny Transparent Round Sunglasses, $225, stellamccartney.com. Your eco warrior friend will love their sustainability, true to the Stella McCartney ethos—and the futuristic feel. The transparent frames are contrasted with blue lenses, with stars to add extra adornment.

Alaia Black Sunglasses, $895, maison-alaia.com. Cher Horowitz would surely approve, and you can also yell that these are Alaia. It’s all about the details with these sophisticated sunnies. The studs are hand-applied to the acetate frames.

Thierry Lasry Gendery, $575, thierrylasry.com. Everyone else around the tree will be green with envy when these unique shades are revealed. Handmade from titanium metal in Japan, they’ll bring any outfit up a notch.

Quay Australia Jaded, $65, quayaustralia.com. The super popular Aussie-based sunglasses line joined forces with none other than JLo to create these shades, which explains their major A-list vibes. The oversized frames will certainly shield you from the sun—and stares from fans.

Michael Kors St. Anton Sunglasses, $209, michaelkors.com. It wouldn’t be the holiday season without a little sparkle, so get the party started with these crystal-bedecked shades. With the cat eye silhouette and metallic chain-link arms, they offer lots to love about them. Plus, the rimless sunnies are super lightweight, making them extra comfy.
