'Oo Baava' promo from 'Prati Roju Pandage' out - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

15 Nov 2019

The promo of the song 'Oo Baava' from 'Pati Roju Pandage' is out.  Composed by Thaman, it's shot on Raashi Khanna and Sai Dharam Tej.  The full song will be out on November 18, the makers say.

The family entertainer will hit the screens on December 20.  Set in a city and also a village, 'PRP' has been written and directed by Maruthi.  Raashi Khanna is the female lead, but more than her, Sathyaraj has an important role.

Produced by Bunny Vas and presented by Allu Aravind, its cinematography is by Vijayabhaskar.  Written by Deva Datta, it has Rao Ramesh in a key role.  Murli Sharma, Ajay, Hari Teja, Satyam Rajesh, Bharath Reddy and Suhas are part of the cast.
