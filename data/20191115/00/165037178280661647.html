Bayer Leverkusen want Todibo on loan

15 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Barca are preparing for the winter transfer market. There are footballer who are hardly playing under Valverde and could leave. According to the French press, Jean-Clair Todibo could be one of them. Bayer Leverkusen want to get him on a loan deal.

So far this season, Todibo has played just two matches. He only started one of them. In total, he has played 77 minutes. Very poor figures which make us think in a more than possible departure. 

The central defender could leave the Camp Nou in the winter transfer window. He will certainly not be short of suitors. 

According to 'Le10Sport', a Bundesliga club are interested in him. Bayer Leverkusen are apparently following the player very closely.

Bayer Leverkusen's proposal is to sign Todibo on loan. They bvelieve the Bundesliga would be a more than interesting option for the defender's growth. 

Todibo needs experience and Barca could opt for that formula. It remains to be seen whether, as this French media outlet points out, Bayer Leverkusen make a formal proposal to the Catalan side to get the Frenchman on loan. 

In the meantime, Todibo is living a dream at Barca. "Three years ago I was in Lilac and now I'm training with Messi, Pique...", the defender said recently.
