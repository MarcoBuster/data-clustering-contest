"Messi has helped me a lot"

15 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Barcelona striker Luis Suarez stated that his team-mate Leo Messi has helped him " a lot" in his "personal growth" since he signed for the team five seasons ago.

"I had a friendship with Leo from the start. From living together, you turn into friends and the moments that you spend with him also show on the pitch," Suarez states in an interview with the media.

The two South American footballers are neighnours in Barcelona (Castelldefels) and have a very strong, personal relationship. "He has shown me companionship many times. He helps me when I was fighting for the Ballon d'Or and he also was. The other day, on the pitch at Eibar, he gifted me a goal," he recalled.

In his sixth season at Barcelona, Luis Suarez has already scored 185 goals, something which puts him amongst the top five goalscorers in the team's history.
