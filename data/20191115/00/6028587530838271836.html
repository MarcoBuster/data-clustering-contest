UFC Fight Night 164 Main Event Preview: Stats, Facts And Numbers For Jan Blachowicz Vs. Ronaldo Souza

15 Nov 2019 by Trent Reinsmith

Jan Blachowicz welcomed former UFC middleweight titleholder Luke Rockhold to the light heavyweight division in a rather unfriendly manner. Blachowicz knocked out Rockhold at the 1:39 mark of the second round of that July fight. After the win, Blachowicz called for a matchup against current UFC light heavyweight kingpin Jon Jones. The UFC opted to deny Blachowicz his request. Instead, he welcomes another long-time middleweight to the 205-pound division when he faces Ronaldo “Jacare” Souza in the main event of Saturday’s UFC Fight Night 164.

Souza, who has alternated wins and losses in his past six fights, is coming off a decision loss to Jack Hermansson in April. Here are some stats, facts and numbers for the upcoming matchup between Rua and Craig.

UFC Fight Night 164 takes place at Ginasio do Ibirapuera in Sao Paulo, Brazil. The entire card streams on ESPN+. Prelims begin at 5 p.m. ET, followed by the main card at 8 p.m. ET.
